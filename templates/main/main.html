About OUR Project --- AI Facial Recognition Website

A website for personal photo categorization and management based on facial recognition technology aims to provide users with an intelligent solution for photo storage, retrieval, and management. The website utilizes a variety of advanced facial recognition algorithms that can automatically identify faces in photos and categorize and tag photos based on the recognition results. This simplifies the complexity of managing a large number of photos for users and meets the needs of photo management in modern digital life.
